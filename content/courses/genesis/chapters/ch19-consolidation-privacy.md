# Chapter 19: UTXO Consolidation & Privacy Risks

## 19.0 When to Consolidate
- Consolidate during low-fee windows; reduce future tx size/cost.

## 19.1 Privacy Erosion Risks & Coin-Control Discipline
- Combining unrelated UTXOs links them; avoid unnecessary merges; use coin control.

## 19.2 Spend Plans for Future Fees and Privacy
- Plan fee windows, keep sensitive UTXOs separate, design spend paths carefully.

### Activity: Draft a Consolidation Plan
- Analyze UTXOs, choose merges, plan future spends; simulate on testnet.
