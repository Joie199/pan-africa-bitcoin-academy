# Chapter 5: The Birth of Bitcoin

## 5.0 The 2008 Financial Crisis Context
Risky lending, defaults, failing banks, and massive bailouts triggered a trust crisis. Savings vanished; money printing spiked.

## 5.1 Satoshi Nakamoto and the Whitepaper
October 31, 2008: “Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System.” Anonymous creator; breakthrough was decentralization and scarcity.

## 5.2 Bitcoin as Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash
- Public ledger (blockchain) replaces banks.
- Private key signs; network verifies; irreversible once confirmed.
- Key traits: decentralization, transparency, fixed supply (21M).
