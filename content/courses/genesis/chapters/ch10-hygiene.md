# Chapter 10: Good Bitcoin Hygiene

## Introduction
Habits protect privacy, security, and control.

## 10.0 New Receive Address Each Time; Verify on Device
- Use fresh addresses to avoid linkage.
- Verify address on device screen to prevent swaps.

## 10.1 Labeling Transactions and UTXOs
- Label sources/uses to aid future spending and audits.

## 10.2 Basic Privacy Habits
- Avoid combining unrelated UTXOs.
- Avoid address reuse; be mindful of breadcrumbs (txids, screenshots).

### Activity: Hygiene Checklist Audit
- Review wallet history: address reuse, labels, UTXO combinations.
